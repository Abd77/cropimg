How it Works?

Catch image inside the box and move it. You can also zoom in or zoom out the photo by the buttons on right side.

Black elements on image, can be used to shors zooming the image or move image to one of the corners, or center.

image taken from http://unsplash.com/

Do i need anything else?

Yes. You need only include jQuery to your document in version 1.9+ or higher. You can find this HERE.
